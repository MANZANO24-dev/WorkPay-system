About the WorkPay System

Manage payroll details, track working hours, and ensure accurate salary calculations with our efficient system.

Our system streamlines payroll management by tracking attendance, calculating salaries, and generating QR codes for seamless check-ins and check-outs.

Roles & Salaries:
Dean: PHP 1,500/day
Faculty: PHP 850/day
Staff: PHP 650/day

Features:

1. User Authentication - Secure login and registration system for employees and admin.

2. Employee Management - Add, update, assign roles (Dean, Faculty, Staff). Generate unique QR codes.

3. Attendance Tracking - Employees check in by scanning a QR code. Logs working hours.

4. Payroll Calculation - Auto salary calculation. Deducts 5% Pag-IBIG and 5% SSS if salary exceeds PHP 30,000.

5. Admin Dashboard - View employee check-ins, working hours, and salary breakdowns.
